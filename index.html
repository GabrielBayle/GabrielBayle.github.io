Welcome

I'm a postdoctoral fellow at GATE (Lyon), working with Marie Claire Villeval.

My research lies in experimental and behavioral economics, with current interests in social dilemmas, social norms dynamics and intergenerational dilemmas through lab and field experiments. I'm also interested in methodology, replication, and open science.

Alongside my research, I'm working with Marie Claire Villeval and Iane Vallanzasca on a new large-scale experimental platform called EXPLAIN.

I am the co-organizer of the GATE-Lab seminar.

Feel free to reach out!
